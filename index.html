How do I find dance classes in my area?
Open the app and use the "Discover" feature. You can filter by dance style, skill level, and change your location to find classes that match your needs. Make sure location services are enabled for the most accurate results.
Can I save classes and content for later?
Yes! Tap the heart icon on any class listing or inspiration content to save it to your favorites. You can access all saved items from your profile tab.
How does the daily practice feature work?
If you upload a new inspiration, save a video of you practicing an inspiration, or add information about a class you took, that will contribute to your streak. Build your streak to stay motivated!
