How do I find dance classes in my area?
Open the app and use the "Discover Classes" feature. You can filter by dance style, skill level, location, and schedule to find classes that match your needs. Make sure location services are enabled for the most accurate results.
Can I save classes and content for later?
Yes! Tap the heart icon on any class listing or inspiration content to save it to your favorites. You can access all saved items from your profile tab.
How does the daily practice feature work?
Set your practice goals in Settings, and we'll send you gentle reminders to practice. You can track your daily practice sessions and build streaks to stay motivated.

How do I connect with other dancers?
Use the Community tab to find dancers in your area, join local dance groups, and discover meetups. You can also connect with classmates from dance studios you've attended.
Is my personal information safe?
We take privacy seriously. You control what information you share, and we never share your personal data with third parties without your consent. Check our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I report inappropriate behavior?
Use the report button on any user profile or message. We review all reports promptly and take appropriate action to maintain a safe, welcoming community.

The app is running slowly or crashing
Try closing and reopening the app. If issues persist, restart your device or check for app updates in the App Store. Make sure you're running iOS 18.0 or later.
I'm not receiving notifications
Check your iPhone Settings > Notifications > Dance App and ensure notifications are enabled. Also verify notification preferences within the app settings.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data. You can also contact us at the email below for assistance.
